# Sorbr — Speed Reading & Comprehension

**Read faster. Absorb deeper.**

Sorbr is a free, open-source speed reading app that helps you get through books, articles, and documents faster — without losing comprehension. It flashes words one at a time at a speed you control, and pauses you at regular checkpoints so you actually remember what you read.

No sign-up. No account. No ads. Works offline. Install it like an app straight from your browser.

🌐 **[sorbr.com](https://sorbr.com)**

---

## Features

### ⚡ Speed Reading (RSVP)
Words are displayed one at a time in the center of the screen — a technique called Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP). Your eyes stay fixed in one place, eliminating the time lost scanning across lines. Read at 200 WPM or push to 800+ once you're comfortable.

### 🧠 Comprehension Checkpoints
Going fast is useless if you don't retain anything. Sorbr automatically pauses every few paragraphs and asks you to write a quick summary in your own words. This active recall technique is one of the most effective ways to lock information into memory.

### 📝 Reading Notes
Every checkpoint summary is saved to your Notes panel. When you're done reading, you have a structured set of notes you can copy, export as a TXT file, or save as a PDF.

### 🔖 Print as Bookmarks or Sticky Notes
Export your notes as printable **bookmarks** (2×6 in) or **sticky notes** (3×3 in) — perfect for physical books or sticking on your desk. No account or external service needed; it all prints directly from your browser.

### 📄 Multiple Input Methods
- **Paste text** directly into the editor
- **Upload a PDF, EPUB, or TXT** file
- **Choose chapters** from multi-chapter documents

### 🎛️ Fully Customizable
- Set your reading speed (100–1000+ WPM)
- Choose chunk size (1–4 words at a time)
- Set checkpoint frequency (every paragraph, half page, full page, or never)

### 📱 Installable PWA
Sorbr is a Progressive Web App — add it to your home screen from any browser and it works just like a native app, including **offline support** after the first visit.

## How to Use

1. **Paste or upload your text** on the main screen
2. **Set your WPM** — 250–300 is a good starting point if you're new to speed reading
3. **Hit Start Reading**
4. Use **Space** to play/pause, **← →** to skip, **↑ ↓** to adjust speed on the fly
5. At each **checkpoint**, write what you remember — then keep going
6. When done, **review your notes** and export or print them

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `Space` | Play / Pause |
| `← →` | Skip back / forward 10 words |
| `↑ ↓` | Speed up / slow down |
| `N` | Open notes panel |

## Install as an App

Visit [sorbr.com](https://sorbr.com) in your browser and look for the install prompt:

- **Chrome / Edge (desktop):** Click the install icon in the address bar
- **Safari (iPhone/iPad):** Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
- **Chrome (Android):** Tap the menu → "Add to Home Screen"

Once installed, Sorbr opens full-screen and works completely offline.
